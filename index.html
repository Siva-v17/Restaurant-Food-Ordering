A kitchen built around one hearth, not a hundred switches.

Every plate that leaves Stackly passes through the same eight-foot hearth — oak, applewood, and citrus embers doing the work that gas never could. Chef Elena Moss and her team break down whole animals daily, forage the coastline for greens, and let the fire decide the menu.
